Hey, my name's David! Originally from California, USA, I moved to Japan in 2022 after first visiting in 2019. After moving here, I worked at an eikaiwa where I taught children the basics of English and helped adults build and share their opinions in a new language. I eventually moved on to teaching at several universities in Japan, where I could really help students build the foundational skills for studying abroad.
